Welcome to Silicon Guest House

Relaxing Pleasure

SiliconTech’s well-appointed, modern guest house provides accommodation to individuals and groups associated with official visits or guests visiting our campus. The guest house has 16 rooms, including four suites, six standard AC rooms, and six non-AC rooms.

Every room has a twin sharing/ double bed with attached bathrooms, an intercom facility, and a refreshment kit for every guest. They come with a flat-screen television with satellite channels, free Wi-Fi, and 24 hours water availability. The guest house has a luggage store and laundry service. Guests get their early morning tea/coffee in the room. Food is available in the Silicon canteen and filtered drinking water in the rooms.
